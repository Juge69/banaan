The games story

FF7 is one of my favorite games of al time, and with good reason! It has some beautiful game design and a really nice story. You play as a character called cloud strife, who was born and raised in a quiet little village called Nibelheim. To impress tifa, a girl nextdoor. He decides to go try out for soldier, wich is bassicaly the games version of the army. But he fails to join rank one soldier, and just turns into a basic infantryman. One day, cloud, zack, and the best soldier called sephiroth go out to inspect a broken reactor. Reactors are the games energy makers, pulling life out of the planet to turn it into energy. Sephiroth, while being there, discovers that he was not made like normal people. Instead, he was made using cells from some alien lady called Jenova. He goes absolutly crazy, setting Nibelheim on fire in the procces. He almost kills cloud and zack, but they manage to survive. They get found by a scientist, and get locked up to get tested on. The scientist who fiends them is the same one who made sephiroth. They manage to escape, but zack gets killed in the procces. Cloud, having seen so much, goes crazy. He forgets all memories of zack, and obtains his memories. Cloud now thinks that he was the rank 1 soldier, like zack. Tifa finds the now mindbroken cloud at a train station, and decides to take care of him. For more info, you'll need to play the game =)

My opinion

I have about 30 hours in FF7, and i enjoy it alot. It is probably my favorite game of all time, and i love all of it. The storyline is interesting, you get really in depth in the character story's. And its design is also really good, having a remake with in my opinion the best design i have ever seen. It is originaly a game from 1989, wich is verry impressive. making a game this beautiful, without a lot of design options is very good! It is nice to see this games community too, wich is still very lively to this day.
