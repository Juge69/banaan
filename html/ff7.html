The games story

FF7 is one of my favorite games of al time, and with good reason! It has some beautiful game design and a really nice story. You play as a character called cloud strife, who was born and raised in a quiet little village called Nibelheim. To impress tifa, a girl nextdoor. He decides to go try out for soldier, wich is basiccaly the games version of the army. But he fails to join rank one soldier, and just turns into a basic infantryman. One day, cloud, zack, and the best soldier called sephiroth go out to inspect a broken reactor. Reactors are the games energy makers, pulling life out of the planet to turn it into energy. Sephiroth, while being there, discovers that he was not made like normal people. Instead, he was made using cells from some alien lady called Jenova. He goes absolutly crazy, setting Nibelheim on fire in the procces. He almost kills cloud and zack, but they manage to survive. They get found by a scientist, and get locked up to get tested on. The scientist who fiends them is the same one who made sephiroth. They manage to escape, but zack gets killed in the procces. Cloud, having seen so much, goes crazy. He forgets all his times with zack, and instead invisions himself AS zack. Cloud now thinks that he was the rank 1 soldier, like zack. Tifa finds the now mindbroken cloud at a train station, and decides to take care of him. For more info, you'll need to play the game =D

The future impact of the game

of all the things ff7 has brought to the future, one of them is better games. Final Fantasy 7 in particular had a big impact on the industry. It has revolutionised the gaming industry, setting a certain goal of what to suspect in games. FF7 was one of the first games that made sure other games would grow together with it. It was setting records, and basically telling other games to "get better or just as good" as itself. And games since then have continued to grow. Look at the remake compared to the original game for example. While the story might not be just as good (in my opinion), the graphics over the years are stunning. No longer are PNG's being used as background fillers, now we modle those fully. Games like ff7, ocarina of time, mario 64... these where what made up most of the gaming industry in that era. I truly wonder what the future will bring, who knows what the graphics might look like in another 10 years or so!
